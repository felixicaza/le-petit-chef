Frequently Asked Questions

What are the cancellation policies?

Once paid, the deposit and purchase price of any 'Le Petit Chef' menus and associated add-ons are non-refundable. Diners can, however, make changes to their dates up to 48 hours before to the original booking date by contacting lepetitchef@thelondoncabaretclub.com or (+44) 2072420002.

Is Le Petit Chef suitable for children?

Yes, Le Petit Chef offers a Junior menu designed for kids up to 12 years old, with child-friendly dishes. Children aged 6 and above are allowed. Please be informed that the Junior menu is exclusively for children up to 12 years old. If you book the children's menu as an adult, you will be asked on-site whether you would prefer the Classic or Premium menu.

I follow a lactose-free or gluten-free diet. Are there any alternatives available?

You are welcome to choose your desired menu and indicate in the "Notes" section that you prefer a gluten-free or lactose-free option.

How long does one show last?

Each dinner show runs for approximately two hours with a 30-minute interval in between time slots. Please come at least 30 minutes before your scheduled show begins so as not to disrupt the experience.

Can everyone seated at a table choose a different menu?

Yes, different menus can be selected. Please note that the same videos are shown to all guests, regardless of which menu you select.

What is Le Petit Chef

Le Petit Chef & Friends is an immersive 3D dining experience hosted by The London Cabaret Club. The show features miniature chefs from around the world, including Le Petit Chef, who present an interactive culinary spectacle right on your plate, blending 3D animation with gourmet dining.

Is there a deposit?

For the majority of our 80+ locations worldwide, we request a deposit of approximately 10% of the selected menu price to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance will be due at the venue on the day of your experience. This deposit helps secure your booking and ensures a seamless dining experience.
